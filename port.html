Football Tournament Database Management System

I made this project from scratch for my 5th sem subject, DBMS. I used HTML5, CSS3, Phpmyadmin and Mysql. I used the XAMPP server to connect the database to the front-end. This project was solely made for the purpose of making tournament management systems simpler. It reduces manual work and is easy to use. Players can register through a team or as individuals. You can register as team with captain's email and giving a name for the team. This website assigns a Team ID itself. It gives the scehdule, the No. of teams, No. of players, Team Stats and Player stats, and also mainly shows the points table.
